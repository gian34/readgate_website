How Readgate works

Readgate uses Apple's Screen Time framework to block selected apps and helps you unlock them after a short reading challenge.

Common fixes

  • Open Readgate and confirm Screen Time permission is enabled.
  • Go to Settings, choose Blocked Apps, and refresh your selection.
  • If blocking does not update, restart Readgate and try selecting the apps again.

Privacy

Your reading history and app blocking selections stay on your device. Readgate does not require an account.
