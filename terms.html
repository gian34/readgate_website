4. Free tier and Pro subscription

Readgate is free to download and use with the following limitations on the free tier:

  • Up to 1 protected app or website
  • Unlimited reading challenge completions
  • Fixed 10 minute break duration
  • 7 days of reading history

Readgate Pro is an auto-renewable subscription that adds:

  • Unlimited protected apps and websites.
  • Flexible break timing from 1 to 60 minutes.
  • Saved reading history beyond the last 7 days.
  • Optional self-imposed daily unlock limits.
