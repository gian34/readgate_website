Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Readgate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Readgate is designed to work entirely on your device. We collect as little data as possible:

  • Reading activity: Articles you read and time spent reading are stored locally on your device only.
  • App blocking preferences: Your list of blocked apps is stored locally using Apple's Screen Time framework and never leaves your device.
  • No account required: Readgate does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

2. Data Storage

All data (reading history, stats, settings) is stored locally on your iPhone using AsyncStorage and Apple's native frameworks. We do not operate any servers that store personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

Readgate fetches article content from the Wikipedia API (wikimedia.org). When the app requests articles, your IP address may be visible to Wikimedia's servers as part of a standard HTTPS request. Please refer to Wikimedia's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Apple Screen Time & Family Controls

Readgate uses Apple's Screen Time framework (Family Controls) to block and unblock apps. This functionality operates entirely on your device and through Apple's system. No app usage data is transmitted to us.

5. Analytics & Crash Reporting

Readgate does not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting services. We do not track your behavior within the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Readgate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@readgateapp.com.
