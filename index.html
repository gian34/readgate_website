Questions

Good to know.

How does Readgate actually block an app?

Readgate uses Apple’s Family Controls, Managed Settings, and Screen Time frameworks. Choose a target, and iOS shows Readgate’s Gate when you try to open it.

Do I have to pay to use the Gate?

No. Free includes one protected app or website and unlimited reading unlocks. Pro adds unlimited targets, schedules, adjustable unlock windows, daily limits, and deeper insights.

Where do the articles come from?

Readgate curates short reads from open knowledge sources such as Wikipedia. Every article links back to its original source.

Can Readgate see the apps I choose?

Readgate needs your on-device selection to run the Gate. Protected app names and website domains are not intentionally sent to Readgate servers, product analytics, or crash reporting.

Which devices are supported?

Readgate is designed for iPhone and supports iOS 15.1 or later.
