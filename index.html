How it works

Three steps to a healthier habit

No willpower needed. Readgate creates friction that actually benefits you.

1

Block your distractions

Choose which apps to block — Instagram, TikTok, X, whatever steals your time. Uses Apple's built-in Screen Time so it actually works.

2

Read a short article

Every time you want to open a blocked app, Readgate shows you a short Wikipedia article. Read it to earn your unlock.

3

Unlock & go

Finish reading and your apps unlock for a few minutes. You get what you wanted — and you learned something new along the way.
