Questions

Good to know.

How does Readgate actually block an app?

Readgate uses Apple’s Family Controls and Managed Settings frameworks. Choose a target, and iOS shows Readgate’s Gate when you try to open it. Readgate does not receive your general Screen Time history.

Do I have to pay to use the Gate?

No. Free includes one protected app or website, unlimited reading unlocks, a 10 minute window, reading history, and weekly stats. Pro brings reading gates to your whole phone, adds your timing and rules, and unlocks the full Learning Profile and full Readgate Originals.

Where do the articles come from?

Readgate mixes bundled Insight Cards and Readgate Originals with curated open knowledge from sources such as Wikipedia and Wikivoyage. Source links are shown where applicable, and bundled content remains available offline.

Can Readgate see the apps I choose?

Your selection stays on your device and is shared locally with Readgate’s iOS extensions. Protected app names and website domains are not intentionally sent to Readgate servers, product analytics, or crash reporting.

Which devices are supported?

Readgate is designed for iPhone and supports iOS 15.1 or later.
