FAQ
Common questions.
Why does Readgate need Screen Time access?

Screen Time (Family Controls) is Apple's only API that lets third-party apps display a system-level shield when you open another app. Without it, Readgate can't intercept a blocked app at all.

Your Screen Time authorization is managed entirely by iOS and never shared with Readgate's servers.

Does Readgate collect personal data?

No personal data — no name, email, or profile. Your sessions, streak, and blocked app selection are stored on your device.

No account, no name, no email. Sessions and streaks live on your device. Readgate contacts Wikipedia for articles and Apple/RevenueCat for purchases. Anonymous crash reporting (Sentry) and usage analytics (PostHog) are used to improve the app — both use a random device ID with no link to your identity.

I finished reading but the app is still blocked. Why?

If you have another Screen Time app installed (such as Opal or Freedom), it manages its own independent shield. Readgate can only remove its own block — it has no access to other apps' shields.

How long is the unlock window?

10 minutes by default. Once the window expires, the shield re-activates automatically. Pro users can set a custom duration in Settings.

Can I change which apps are blocked?

Yes. Open Readgate, go to Settings → Manage Blocked Apps, and use Apple's native picker to add or remove apps at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

The shield doesn't appear when I open a blocked app.

Family Controls requires a physical iPhone running iOS 18 or later. The shield does not work in the iOS Simulator, on iPad, or on Mac.

Make sure Readgate is authorized: go to Settings → Screen Time → Readgate on your device and confirm access is enabled.

My streak disappeared. What happened?

Streaks are calculated from completed reading sessions stored locally. If you reinstalled Readgate or cleared app data, the history is gone. Streaks also reset if you miss a full calendar day.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free users can protect 1 app or website with unlimited reading unlocks. Pro adds unlimited protected apps, custom unlock settings, and full reading history.
